RANGE OFFICER COMMANDS WILL BE IMMEDIATELY OBEYED

    Shooters will always strictly follow Cooper's Four Fundamental Rules of Firearm Safety

  1. All Firearms are always loaded
  2. Never point the muzzle of a firearm at anything you are not willing to destroy
  3. Finger must be off the trigger, and outside the trigger guard until sights are on target, and you intend to fire
  4. Be sure of your target, what is around it, and what is behind it

NO FIREARMS HANDLING EXCEPT IN A SHOOTING LANE AND FORWARD OF THE RED SAFETY LINE

ANY FIREARM REMOVED FROM A CASE, HOLSTER, OR CARRY TUB OUTSIDE OF THE RANGE MUST BE SHOWN CLEAR IMMEDIATELY BY RANGE STAFF

  1. 15 yard pistol bays are PISTOL CALIBER ONLY
    • .22 Rifles and Pistol Caliber Carbines are allowed, notify Range Staff at Check-In
  2. 25 yard Tactical Bay is for pistols, rifles, and shotguns
    • Shotguns - Notify Range Staff at Check-In
    • Slugs and Buckshot Only
    • Must have shoulder stock and sights
  3. 50 Yard Rifle Bay is RIFLES ONLY
  4. Firearms must be in a case, carry tub, or holster at all times for transportation in the facility and to and from vehicles
  5. No Children under 12 on the range
    • All minors must have adult supervision at all times
  6. A valid, physical, government issued photo ID is required to be on the range
  7. No food or drink on the range
  8. No pregnant women
  9. No consumption of any substance that impairs normal use of physical or mental abilities
  10. Eye and Ear Protection is required at all times inside the range
  11. No horseplay. No irresponsible, discourteous or negligent behavior
  12. Handguns and handgun ammunition can only be rented or sold to individuals 21 and older
  13. Rifles and rifle ammunition can only be rented or sold to individuals 18 and older
  14. No returns on Gift Certificates, Ammunition, Firearms, Optics, or NFA (Class 3) items
  1. It is the firers sole responsibility to understand safe operation of their firearm and ensure that their firearm is in safe working order
  2. Do not point firearms at facility equipment, such as target carriers, tables, walls - except target and rubber berm beyond target
  3. Do not shoot at targets intended for other lanes
  4. Firearms must be in a case, carry tub, or holster at all times except when in a shooting lane, and forward of the red safety line
    • Notify a Range Safety Officer to clear any type of malfunction
    • Do not remove a malfunctioned firearm from it's lane, and it must remain pointed downrange at all times
    • Do not transfer firearms from lane to lane, or person to person, without a case, carry tub, or holster
  5. Wash hands in D-Lead soap (available in bathrooms) immediately after shooting to remove lead dust
  6. Rapid Fire is at Range Safety Officer Discretion
    • Firearms must be under control at all times, with all shots landing on the target
    • Notify Range Safety Officer before commencing rapid, binary, or automatic fire
    • Firer must cease rapid, binary, or automatic fire immediately upon Range Safety Officer Command
  7. NO HOLSTER DRAWS
  8. All firearms must have sights, which must be utilized by the firer
  9. Shotguns must have a shoulder stock
    If a CEASE FIRE is called by Range Staff:
  1. Firers must unload and show clear all firearms
  2. Firearms must be placed on tables, with the action open, and muzzles pointed downrange
  3. Firers must step behind the RED SAFETY LINE until the range is returned to HOT status by the Range Safety Officer
  4. Do not remove eye or ear protection
    1. No aluminum cased ammunition
    • Steel, brass, and nickel-plated brass are OK
    2. No steel core or 'black tip' armor piercing rounds
    • Green tip is OK
  3. No incendiary, explosive, or tracer rounds
    4. Shotguns: No bird shot or target loads
    • Must be 00 buckshot or slugs
  5. Maximum Caliber: 50AE/BMG
  6. Bore diameter larger than .480", handguns producing above 1700 foot pounds of kinetic energy, or rifles producing above 3300 foot pounds of kinetic energy must be approved by Range Staff at Check-In
