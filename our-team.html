E. A. Travis, JD

CEO

eatravis@texaslegendsguns.com

Mike McLoughlin

Firearms Instructor

mikem@texaslegendsguns.com

Mike was raised in North Texas and retired from a local DOD contractor after a career of 41 years. He is an avid hunter, gun collector and a shooting sports enthusiast. Mike has hundreds of hours of specialized training in defensive and tactical handgun and rifle for safety, operation and marksmanship. Mike is an NRA certified Handgun instructor and Range Safety Officer, and holds an NRA certification to teach the “Refuse to be a Victim” course. Mike holds a license from the Texas DPS as a classroom and firearms instructor for Private Security Level III and is certified to teach the Texas License to Carry course. At Texas Legends, Mike provides training for all skill levels through classroom and private instruction to individuals, couples, and small groups. Mike teaches the “Introduction”, “Intermediate” and “Advanced” handgun courses. Mike has a passion for both classroom and range training to assist shooters in reaching and exceeding their personal goals.

Jose Primera

Firearms Instructor

Dwight Bridges

Firearms Instructor

Dwight is an NRA Certified Range Safety Officer (RSO) at Texas Legends. His father started teaching him firearm safety in elementary school with his first Daisy BB gun. Soon after he graduated to his dad’s Winchester model 74 .22 caliber rifle. In the 9th grade, he began sharing this skill with others when he started helping run rifle ranges at summer camp. Over the years he has taught hundreds of people to shoot safely and helped many of them improve their skill levels. He also continues to practice improving his own firearm skills and add new ones. His latest new skill being an interest in long range shooting. With over three decades of experience teaching leadership skills in a wilderness environment as a hobby, it was a natural move to pursue this in retirement. So, after retiring from a rewarding telecommunications career in 2016, he began a venture teaching outdoor living and survival skills. When not at the range, Dwight and his wife enjoy travelling, hiking, fine cuisine, spending time with friends, and taking their two dogs on regular walks.
