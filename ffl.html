FFL Transfers In

Purchasing a firearm online or out of state? We've got you covered!

$35

Without Texas License to Carry

$25

With Texas License to Carry

The Process

  1. Order your firearm, designating Texas Legends in Allen, TX as your local FFL Dealer
    2. Your Seller will Need
    • Our FFL License - requested by emailing ffl@texaslegendsguns.com
    • Our Physical Address
    • 1499 Central Expressway North
    • Allen, TX 75013
    3. Your Seller Ships the Firearm to us using UPS or FedEx
    • They should include in the box a copy of their FFL and the customer contact information
    4. We receive the firearm
    • Typically between 1 and 3 PM excluding Sundays
    5. We process the firearm into our FFL Acquisition and Disposition books
    • Typically takes 2-4 hours, but can be up to 24
    6. We will call you when your order is ready for pickup!
    • If voicemail box is full, or not set up, we will attempt to email if we have an email address on file, or if it is included with the firearm
    7. Upon Arrival, notify Range Staff that you have an FFL transfer to pick up
        You will need a valid photo ID and proof of Texas Residency
      • A valid Texas Driver's License serves as both
          • Residency can be verified ONLY using government issued documents, which must:
      • Contain a name that matches your photo ID
      • A complete Texas address
      • Be issued by a city or state government
          • Examples
        • City Utility Bill (NOT an apartment or neighborhood issued bill)
        • Texas Motor Vehicle Registration (NOT a renewal notice)
        • Voter Registration
    8. Fill Out a ATF 4473 Firearms Transaction Record
        This includes a NICS (National Instant Criminal Search) background check
      • A Texas License to Carry with address matching proof of TX residency can be used in lieu of NICS check
      • NICS checks can take 15-45 minutes to return either Proceed, Deny, or Delay
          • If delayed, the firearm can be released to you after 5 BUSINESS days
        • Business Days exclude federal holidays and weekends
    • Individuals under 21 must undergo a mandatory 10 business day waiting period
    • Individuals returning to complete a 4473 on a day other than the initial day must re-certify that the form is still true
  9. Pay the transfer fee and receive your Transfer Out Receipt
  10. Take Home your Firearm! Bring it back to shoot with us!

Class 3 (NFA) Items

The approval process for NFA items can be confusing! Let us help!

We have partnered with Silencer Shop to handle all your Class 3 (NFA) needs!

We keep devices pending approval on premises for your use on the range while you wait!

The Process

    1. Order your Suppressor or Short Barrel Rifle from SilencerShop.com
    • Set Texas Legends as your dealer
      • We can also accept Class 3 devices from any dealer
      • $100 NFA transfer fee applies on pickup after approval on ALL items purchased at other dealers
      • We will input item into SilencerShop's dashboard to streamline steps
      • We receive the device typically within 10-14 Days
      • Pending ATF Form 3 Approval
    2. Set up account with SilencerShop and the ATF's eForms portal
    • Use the same email address for both
  3. Come in Store to complete fingerprinting and paperwork at our SilencerShop kiosk
  4. Purchase $200 federal tax stamp from SilencerShop
  5. We will prepare your Form 4
    6. Certification of Form 4 over the phone with one of our NFA managers (ask for Noah or Robert)
    • Requires a desktop computer
    • ATF eForms 4 digit PIN
  7. We submit your Form 4
  8. ATF approval period - averages 9-10 months
    9. You will receive an email from the ATF upon approval, come in store for final pickup!
    • Fill out a 4473 (Firearms Transaction Record)
  10. Take home your device! Bring it back to shoot with us!
