Range Rules

General

  1. Eye and Ear protection must be worn at all times
  2. No Pregnant Women
  3. No Children under age 12 on the range
  4. No Food or Drink on the Range
  5. No Aluminum cased ammunition
  6. When entering and exiting range, only open 1 door at a time
  7. Used targets are disposed of laid flat on back range tables

Firearms Handling

  1. All Firearms must remain in cases, carry tubs, or holsters until in a lane and forward of the RED SAFETY LINE
  2. Do NOT transfer firearms from lane to lane, or person to person without a case, carry tub, or holster
  3. All Firearm muzzles must be pointed downrange at all times
  4. NO HOLSTER DRAWS
  5. NO RAPID FIRE (Per Range Safety Officer Discretion)

Shotguns

  1. Only buckshot or slugs - NO birdshot or target loads
  2. Must have a shoulder stock and sights

Rifles

  1. Must have sights
  2. 50 yard Rifle range is Rifles only
